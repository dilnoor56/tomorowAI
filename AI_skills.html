Companies Taking help of AI

From Business Insider India and discusses Marvel’s “Secret Invasion” series . It mentions that the series used AI to create its intro sequence, which has led to some fans being upset. This highlights the ongoing debate about the use of AI in creative processes and how it can impact audience reactions.


-AI in Creative Processes: AI can significantly speed up the creative process, allowing for quicker production times and potentially reducing costs. This can be particularly useful in creating complex visual effects or sequences.

- Marvel’s Approach Experimentation: Marvel’s decision to use AI for the intro sequence of “Secret Invasion” can be seen as an experiment to explore new possibilities in storytelling and production.

Overall, the use of AI in entertainment is a complex and multifaceted issue,  with both potential benefits and drawbacks. It’s a topic that will likely continue to evolve as technology advances and as creators and audiences navigate the balance between innovation and tradition.
