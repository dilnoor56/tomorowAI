 The article from ‘The Indian Express’ discusses Air India’s significant investment in modernizing its digital systems. The airline has made an initial investment of $200 million to integrate a GPT-driven chatbot, which is part of their broader initiative to enhance their digital infrastructure.

Competitive Edge:Investing in advanced AI technology positions Air India as a forward-thinking airline, potentially giving it a competitive edge in the market. This move can attract tech-savvy customers who value innovative services.
